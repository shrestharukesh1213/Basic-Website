Book List

This is a list of the most popular books in our library. These books have been rated highly by our users and are sure to provide you with hours of enjoyment.

The Alchemist by Paulo Coelho: This is a story about a young shepherd who travels from his homeland in Spain to the Egyptian desert in search of treasure buried near the Pyramids. Along the way, he meets a variety of people who teach him about life and the importance of following one's dreams.

To Kill a Mockingbird by Harper Lee: This is a classic coming-of-age story set in the American South during the Great Depression. The story follows a young girl named Scout Finch as she witnesses the trial of a black man accused of raping a white woman.

The Lord of the Rings by J.R.R. Tolkien: This is a fantasy trilogy about a hobbit named Frodo Baggins who is tasked with destroying the One Ring, an evil artifact created by the Dark Lord Sauron.

Harry Potter and the Sorcerer's Stone by J.K. Rowling: This is the first book in the Harry Potter series about a young boy who discovers that he is a wizard. He is sent to Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry, where he learns about magic and makes friends with Ron Weasley and Hermione Granger.

The Hunger Games by Suzanne Collins: This is a dystopian novel about a young woman named Katniss Everdeen who volunteers to take her younger sister's place in a deadly competition. The competition is called the Hunger Games, and it is a televised event in which 24 teenagers must fight to the death.

The Book Thief by Markus Zusak: This is a historical novel set in Nazi Germany during World War II. The story is told from the perspective of Death, who narrates the story of a young girl named Liesel Meminger who is sent to live with foster parents. Liesel learns to read and steals books, which she shares with her foster father.
